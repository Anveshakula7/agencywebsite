How We Work

Our Video Creation Process

From brief to final video — a simple, fast, and transparent process every time.

1
Share Your Brief

Tell us your business, video goal, target audience, preferred language, and any references you like. A 5-minute WhatsApp chat is all we need to get started.

2
Script Writing & Approval

We write a compelling script tailored to your product and audience. You review and approve before we start generating — so the video says exactly what you want.

3
AI Video Generation

Our AI tools generate scenes, transitions, animations, and visuals based on your script and brand. This is where the magic happens — studio-quality output in hours, not days.

4
Voiceover & Music

We add a professional AI or human voiceover in your chosen language and pair it with the perfect royalty-free background music to set the right mood and tone.

5
Branding, Captions & CTAs

We integrate your logo, brand colors, subtitles, and call-to-action overlays — making the video look completely professional and on-brand, ready to post or run as an ad.

6
Review & Revisions

You receive the first draft within 24 hours. Watch it, share your feedback, and we'll make up to 2 rounds of revisions until you're completely happy with the final video.

7
Final Delivery in All Formats

We deliver the final video in all required formats — landscape (YouTube/ads), portrait (Reels/Shorts), and square (Feed) — in HD or 4K. Ready to post, run as an ad, or embed on your website.

Got Questions?

Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you want to know before ordering your AI video.

How is AI video different from normal video production? +
Traditional video needs cameras, crew, actors, studios, and days of editing — costing ₹20,000 to ₹2 lakhs+. AI video uses advanced AI tools to generate professional visuals, animations, and voiceovers in hours — at a fraction of the cost. The output quality is stunning and often indistinguishable from traditional production.
How long does it take to deliver a video? +
Most videos are delivered within 24 hours of brief confirmation. Complex videos with custom AI avatars or multiple scenes may take 48 hours. Growth and Dominator pack clients get priority delivery within 12 hours. We always share a timeline upfront so you know exactly when to expect your video.
Can you make videos in Telugu or Hindi? +
Yes! We create videos with voiceovers and on-screen text in Telugu, Hindi, or English — or any combination. For local Hyderabad businesses, Telugu-language videos often perform significantly better with local audiences on Instagram and YouTube.
Can I use the video for paid ads on Facebook/Instagram/YouTube? +
Absolutely! All our AI videos are optimized for paid advertising. We can even create specific ad variations — hook cuts, shorter versions for Stories, CTA-focused versions — so you have everything you need to run effective campaigns right out of the box.
Do I own the video after delivery? +
Yes — 100% full ownership. Once delivered and paid for, the video is entirely yours. You can use it on any platform, run it as an ad, put it on your website, share on WhatsApp — forever, with no licensing fees or restrictions.
What do I need to provide to get started? +
Very little! Just share your business name, what you want to promote, your target audience, preferred language, and any reference videos you like. We handle the script, visuals, voiceover, music, and editing. If you have your logo and brand colors — even better, but not essential to start.
How many revisions are included? +
All packages include 2 free revision rounds. Growth and Dominator pack clients get unlimited revisions. We work until you're completely happy with the final video — your satisfaction is non-negotiable for us.
Can I get a free sample before ordering? +
Yes! WhatsApp Anvesh with your business details and we'll share relevant sample videos we've created for similar businesses. For serious enquiries, we can even create a short sample clip specifically for your business so you can see the quality before committing.
