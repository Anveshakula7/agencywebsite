How We Work

Our Ad Campaign Process

From strategy to results — a transparent step-by-step process that delivers every time.

1
Free Ad Audit & Goal Setting

We start with a free audit of your existing Facebook page & ad performance (if any), then set clear KPIs — whether your goal is leads, sales, footfall, or brand awareness in Hyderabad.

2
Audience Research & Strategy

We identify your ideal customer profile — demographics, interests, behaviors, income level — and build a targeting strategy using interest audiences, custom audiences, and lookalikes.

3
Ad Creative & Copy Production

Our team designs scroll-stopping visuals and writes high-converting ad copy for each variant — images, carousels, and short videos — to test what converts best for your audience.

4
Campaign Setup & Launch

We set up campaigns with the correct objective, ad set targeting, placements, bidding strategy, and budget — then launch and monitor closely in the first 48 hours for issues.

5
Ongoing Optimization & A/B Testing

Every week we analyze performance, kill underperforming ads, scale winning ones, test new creatives and audiences, and refine targeting to continuously lower your cost per lead.

6
Lead Management & Follow-Up

Leads from forms are immediately shared with you via WhatsApp or Google Sheet. We also help set up auto-reply WhatsApp messages so every lead gets an instant response.

7
Monthly Reporting & Next Month Planning

Every month you get a clear report — impressions, clicks, leads, cost per lead & ROAS. We review it together and plan the next month's strategy to beat the previous results.

Got Questions?

Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know before starting your Facebook Ads campaign.

How much should I spend on Facebook Ads? +
For most Hyderabad businesses, we recommend starting with ₹5,000–₹15,000 per month in ad spend. This generates enough data and leads to prove ROI. As campaigns perform, we scale the budget. Our management fee is separate from ad spend, which goes directly to Meta.
How quickly will I see results from Facebook Ads? +
Most clients start seeing leads within 3–5 days of launch. The first 2 weeks are a learning phase where we test audiences and creatives. Results improve significantly in weeks 3–4 as we optimize on real data. Month 2 is typically when the best results come in.
Do I need a website to run Facebook Ads? +
No! Facebook's instant lead forms capture leads inside the app. We also run Click-to-WhatsApp ads that send people directly to your WhatsApp chat. A website helps with retargeting and pixel tracking but is not mandatory to start getting leads.
What's the difference between boosting a post and running proper Facebook Ads? +
Boosting is a limited option with basic targeting and no optimization — it wastes budget. Professional Ads via Ads Manager give you precise audience targeting, proper campaign objectives, pixel tracking, retargeting, A/B testing, and full optimization — far more efficient use of every rupee.
Can you run ads in Telugu or Hindi? +
Absolutely! We create ad copy and visuals in Telugu, Hindi, or English — whichever your target audience responds to best. For local Hyderabad businesses, Telugu creatives often outperform English significantly in click-through rates and conversions.
Will my ad account get banned? +
Only if Meta's policies are violated. We strictly follow all advertising guidelines — no misleading claims or prohibited content. We've managed 300+ campaigns and have never had a client account permanently banned. We also review all ad copy and creatives for compliance before publishing.
How do I receive the leads from Facebook Ads? +
Leads from Facebook forms are shared in real-time via WhatsApp or an auto-updated Google Sheet. We can also integrate with CRMs like Zoho or HubSpot. Click-to-WhatsApp leads arrive directly in your WhatsApp with a pre-filled message so you know what they're interested in.
Do you offer a free audit of my current ads? +
Yes! If you're already running Facebook Ads, we'll audit your current campaigns for free — reviewing targeting, creative quality, campaign structure, and wasted spend. We'll identify exactly where your budget is leaking and what changes will improve results, before you commit to anything.
