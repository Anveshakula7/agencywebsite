How We Work

Our Facebook Ads Process

A proven system to get your ads generating leads within the first week of going live.

1
Free Ads Audit & Strategy Call

We review your current ads (if any), understand your business goals, and define your ideal customer. You'll get clear insights on what's working, what's not, and what to do next — before spending a single rupee.

2
Audience & Competitor Research

We identify your best-fit audience segments using Meta's targeting data — interests, behaviours, lookalikes, and demographics. We also spy on your top competitors' active ads using Meta Ad Library.

3
Ad Creative & Copy Production

Our design team creates scroll-stopping visuals and we write persuasive ad copy tailored to your audience's pain points and desires. You approve before anything goes live — always.

4
Campaign Setup & Launch

We structure your Meta Ads account properly — campaign objectives, ad sets, placements, budgets, and bid strategies — and launch your first campaigns. Facebook Pixel and conversion tracking set up from day one.

5
Daily Monitoring & Optimization

We monitor your campaigns daily — cutting underperforming ads, scaling winners, adjusting bids, and refreshing creatives before ad fatigue sets in. Your budget is never left unattended.

6
Retargeting & Funnel Building

We build retargeting campaigns for warm audiences — people who visited your website, watched your videos, or engaged with your page. These campaigns have the lowest CPL and highest conversion rates.

7
Monthly Report & Review

Every month you get a detailed report — total leads generated, cost per lead, ROAS, best-performing ads, and the strategy for next month. You'll always know exactly what your money is doing.

Got Questions?

Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you want to know before starting your Facebook Ads campaign.

How much ad budget do I need to start? +
We recommend a minimum ad budget of ₹5,000–₹10,000 per month to see meaningful results. This is separate from our management fee and paid directly to Meta (Facebook). For competitive industries like real estate and clinics, ₹15,000–₹30,000/month gets significantly better results. We'll recommend the right budget for your goals.
How quickly will I start getting leads? +
Unlike SEO, Facebook Ads can start generating leads within 24–72 hours of going live. The first 7–14 days is a learning phase where Meta's algorithm optimizes delivery. You'll typically see the cost per lead drop and lead quality improve from week 2 onwards as the campaign matures.
Do you handle the ad creative design too? +
Yes — design and copywriting are fully included in our packages. We create static image ads, carousels, and can produce short Reels-style videos depending on your plan. You approve all creatives before they go live. We refresh creatives monthly to prevent ad fatigue.
Do I need a website to run Facebook Ads? +
No. Facebook's native Lead Ad format captures leads directly inside the app — no website required. Customers fill in their name and phone number without ever leaving Facebook or Instagram. This is actually the most popular format for businesses in India because of its simplicity and low friction.
How do you measure campaign success? +
We track leads generated, cost per lead (CPL), click-through rate (CTR), reach, frequency, and ROAS (for e-commerce). Every month you receive a clear report showing these numbers with a comparison to the previous month, along with what we're doing to improve them.
Can you run ads on Instagram too? +
Yes! Facebook and Instagram are both owned by Meta and managed through the same Ads Manager. Our campaigns typically run across both platforms simultaneously, optimizing for where your specific audience is most active. You'll see results from both in a single report.
What if my ads aren't getting results? +
We monitor campaigns daily and proactively fix underperformance. If an ad isn't working, we test new creatives, adjust targeting, or restructure the campaign — we don't wait for a monthly review. You'll always be informed about what's happening and what we're changing to improve results.
Can I cancel my plan anytime? +
Our plans have a minimum 2-month commitment (campaigns need time to exit the learning phase and optimize). After that, cancel with 15 days notice. We don't tie you into long contracts — we earn your continued business by delivering consistent results every month.
