How We Work

Our Google Ads Process

A structured, transparent process designed to get your campaigns live, optimized, and profitable fast.

1
Free Google Ads Audit

If you're already running ads, we audit your account — identifying wasted spend, poor Quality Scores, missing negative keywords, and conversion tracking gaps. If you're starting fresh, we assess your website, competition, and goals to build the right foundation.

2
Keyword Research & Competitor Analysis

We research the highest-intent, most profitable keywords for your business — including long-tail variations that convert at lower cost. We also analyse what keywords your competitors are bidding on and use that intelligence to position you better.

3
Campaign Strategy & Structure

We design your campaign architecture — ad groups, match types, bidding strategy, budget allocation, and targeting settings. A well-structured account means higher Quality Scores, lower CPCs, and better ad positions from day one.

4
Ad Copy & Creative Production

We write multiple ad variations with compelling headlines and descriptions tailored to each keyword group. For Display and YouTube campaigns, we design the visuals and produce the video assets. All copy is A/B tested from launch.

5
Campaign Launch & Tracking Setup

We set up Google Conversion Tracking, link Google Analytics, configure call tracking, and launch your campaigns. Every click, call, and form fill is tracked back to the exact keyword and ad that drove it — from day one.

6
Daily Optimization

We monitor your campaigns every day — pausing underperformers, scaling winners, adding negative keywords, adjusting bids, and testing new ad copy. Google Ads requires daily attention to keep ROI climbing and prevent budget waste.

7
Remarketing & Funnel Expansion

We build remarketing campaigns to re-engage website visitors who didn't convert — keeping your brand visible as they continue researching. We also expand to Display, YouTube, or Shopping campaigns as the data supports it.

8
Monthly Report & Strategy Review

Every month you receive a clear report — total spend, leads generated, cost per lead, ROAS, top keywords, best ads, and the plan for next month. A strategy call is included so you always understand exactly what your money is doing.

Got Questions?

Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you want to know before starting your Google Ads campaign.

How much ad budget do I need for Google Ads? +
We recommend a minimum ad budget of ₹10,000–₹15,000 per month to see meaningful results, depending on your industry. Competitive sectors like real estate, hospitals, and legal services require ₹25,000–₹50,000+ per month. This is paid directly to Google — separate from our management fee. We'll recommend the right budget based on your keywords' average CPC.
How quickly will I start getting leads from Google Ads? +
Google Search Ads can start generating leads within hours of going live — this is the main advantage over SEO. The first 7–14 days is a learning and optimization phase. By week 2, we've gathered enough data to optimize bids and ad copy, and CPL typically drops significantly from week 3 onwards.
What's the difference between Google Ads and SEO? +
Google Ads gives you instant visibility at the top of Google — but you pay per click, and results stop when you stop spending. SEO builds organic rankings that generate free traffic long-term — but takes 3–6 months to show results. We recommend Google Ads for immediate leads and SEO for long-term growth. The best strategy uses both together.
Do you take a percentage of my ad budget? +
No. We charge a flat monthly management fee — not a percentage of your ad spend. You pay Google directly through your own billing account. This means we have zero incentive to inflate your budget. Our goal is the best ROI for you, not the highest spend.
Do I need a website to run Google Ads? +
Yes, Google Search and Display Ads typically require a landing page or website to send traffic to. However, you don't need a large or expensive website — even a simple, well-optimized single-page landing page can work very effectively. We can recommend or build a landing page if needed.
How do you prevent my budget from being wasted on irrelevant clicks? +
We build an extensive negative keyword list from day one — blocking searches that are unlikely to convert (wrong location, wrong intent, competitors researching, etc.). We also use exact and phrase match keywords strategically, and monitor the Search Terms report daily to add new negatives. This typically reduces wasted spend by 30–50%.
How do you track leads and prove the ads are working? +
We set up Google Conversion Tracking to track every form submission, phone call, and purchase — tied back to the exact keyword and ad that drove it. You'll see in your monthly report: total leads, cost per lead, which keywords performed, which ads had the best CTR, and your overall ROAS. Full transparency, always.
Can I cancel my Google Ads management plan anytime? +
Our plans have a minimum 2-month commitment — campaigns need time to exit Google's learning phase and be properly optimized. After that, you can cancel with 15 days notice. Your Google Ads account belongs to you — we'll hand over full access if you ever leave. No lock-in, no hostage accounts.
