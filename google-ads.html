How We Work

Our Google Ads Process

A step-by-step process that launches your Google Ads fast and optimizes them every single week.

1
Free Google Ads Audit

We audit your existing Google Ads account (if any) — identifying wasted spend, low Quality Scores, poor keyword match types, and missed opportunities. If you're starting fresh, we audit your competitors instead.

2
Keyword & Competitor Research

We research 300–500+ keywords your ideal customers are searching for in Hyderabad — sorted by search volume, intent, and CPC. We also analyze what your competitors are bidding on and reverse-engineer what's working for them.

3
Campaign Structure & Ad Copywriting

We build a tightly structured campaign with themed ad groups, write 3–5 ad variations per group, set up ad extensions (callouts, sitelinks, call, location), and create compelling headlines that maximize your click-through rate.

4
Conversion Tracking Setup

We install Google Tag Manager and configure conversion tracking for form submissions, phone calls, WhatsApp clicks, and purchases — so every lead is tracked back to the exact keyword and ad that generated it.

5
Campaign Launch

We launch your campaign and monitor it daily for the first week — catching any early issues with search terms, bids, or quality scores and fixing them immediately before budget is wasted.

6
Weekly Optimization

Every week we review search term reports, add negative keywords, pause underperforming ads, test new ad copy, adjust bids by device and time, and reallocate budget to the campaigns generating the cheapest leads.

7
Monthly Reporting & Strategy Review

Every month you receive a detailed report — clicks, impressions, CTR, cost per click, conversions, cost per lead, and ROAS. We review it together on a call and plan the next month's optimizations to keep improving ROI.

Got Questions?

Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know before starting your Google Ads campaign with Anvesh Digital.

How much Google Ads budget do I need to start? +
For most local Hyderabad businesses, a minimum of ₹10,000–15,000/month ad budget is needed to gather enough data and generate meaningful leads. For competitive industries like real estate or healthcare, ₹25,000–50,000/month is recommended. This budget goes directly to Google — separate from our management fee. We'll recommend the right budget based on your industry and goals.
How fast will I start getting leads from Google Ads? +
Google Search Ads can generate leads from day 1 of going live — that's the power of PPC. However, the first 2–4 weeks is a learning phase where we optimize keywords, bids, and ads based on real data. Most clients see consistent, quality leads by week 3–4, with costs improving month over month as we optimize further.
Do I have full access to my Google Ads account? +
Absolutely — always. We manage your campaigns inside your own Google Ads account. You have admin access at all times and can see every click, every keyword, and every rupee spent in real time through Google Ads and Google Analytics dashboards. Full transparency, always.
What is the difference between management fee and ad budget? +
Your ad budget goes directly to Google — it's what pays for the actual clicks and leads. Our management fee pays for our team's expertise: keyword research, ad writing, daily monitoring, weekly optimization, conversion tracking, and monthly reporting. We never take a percentage of your ad budget — our fee is fixed so we're aligned with getting you results, not spending more of your money.
Can you take over and fix my existing Google Ads account? +
Yes — this is one of our most common requests. We audit your existing account, identify wasted spend, poor keyword structure, low Quality Scores, and missing conversion tracking, then rebuild and optimize it. Most clients see 30–50% reduction in cost per lead within 60 days of us taking over their existing campaigns.
Do you target only Hyderabad or other cities too? +
We can target any location — Hyderabad, specific areas within Hyderabad (Banjara Hills, Jubilee Hills, Gachibowli, etc.), Telangana state, pan-India, or even international. Location targeting is set based on where your customers are. Most of our clients are local Hyderabad businesses, but we manage national campaigns too.
Will you also improve my landing page for better conversions? +
Yes — every package includes landing page CRO (Conversion Rate Optimization) recommendations. We review your landing page and give specific feedback on what to change to improve conversion rate. More conversions from the same traffic means lower cost per lead. On the Dominator plan, we can also build a dedicated landing page for your campaigns.
Can I pause or cancel my Google Ads management anytime? +
Our plans require a minimum 2-month commitment (Google Ads need time to optimize and show real results). After that, you can cancel with 30 days' notice. Your Google Ads account remains yours — we never hold your account hostage. We believe in earning your continued business through consistent results month after month.
