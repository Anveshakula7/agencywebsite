Got Questions?

Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you want to know before starting your SEO campaign.

How long does SEO take to show results? +
SEO is a long-term investment. Most businesses start seeing keyword movement in 4–8 weeks, and significant traffic & lead growth in 3–6 months. The timeline depends on your competition, website age, and current domain authority. We share realistic expectations from day one.
Is SEO better than Google Ads? +
Both have their role. Google Ads gives instant visibility but stops the moment you stop paying. SEO builds lasting organic rankings that keep generating free traffic for months and years. We recommend SEO for long-term growth and Google Ads for immediate leads — ideally both together.
Do you guarantee Page 1 rankings? +
No ethical SEO agency can guarantee exact rankings — Google's algorithm has 200+ factors and changes frequently. What we guarantee is consistent effort, best practices, and transparent monthly reporting showing measurable improvement. Our track record speaks for itself — 50+ businesses ranked on Page 1.
Do you use white hat or black hat SEO? +
100% white hat SEO only. We follow Google's guidelines strictly — no spammy backlinks, keyword stuffing, or shortcuts that can get your site penalized. Our rankings are built to last, not disappear after the next Google update.
How do you measure SEO success? +
We track keyword rankings weekly, organic traffic monthly via Google Analytics, and leads/conversions from organic channels. Every month you receive a detailed report with all these numbers, plus the work done and the plan for the next month.
Do you need access to my website? +
Yes, for on-page and technical SEO we need backend access (WordPress admin, cPanel, or FTP). We treat your credentials with complete confidentiality and only make changes after discussing them with you. You're in control at all times.
Can I cancel my SEO plan anytime? +
Our plans have a minimum 3-month commitment (SEO needs time to show results), after which you can cancel with 30 days notice. We don't lock you into long contracts — we earn your continued business through results.
Do you offer a free SEO audit? +
Yes! We offer a free basic SEO audit for all new clients. We analyze your website's health, current keyword rankings, technical issues, and competitor gaps — then share a clear action plan. No obligation, completely free.
