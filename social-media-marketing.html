How We Work

Our Social Media Process

A transparent, step-by-step social media strategy built for consistent growth and engagement.

1
Free Social Media Audit

We analyze your existing social profiles, content performance, follower quality, competitor accounts, and market positioning — so we know exactly where to start and what to fix first.

2
Brand & Audience Research

We study your target audience — age, location, interests, behaviors, and what content they engage with most. We also define your brand voice so every post sounds uniquely like your business.

3
Content Strategy & Calendar

We build a 30-day content calendar with post types, topics, captions, and hashtags — balanced between promotional, educational, and entertaining content to maximize reach and engagement.

4
Content Creation & Design

Our creative team designs on-brand graphics, edits reels, writes captions, and crafts stories — all reviewed and approved by you before going live. No surprises, ever.

5
Scheduling & Publishing

We post at optimal times when your audience is most active — ensuring maximum organic reach for every post. Consistent daily or weekly cadence to keep your algorithm ranking high.

6
Community Engagement

We monitor comments, DMs, and mentions — responding promptly to build genuine relationships with your followers and turn casual viewers into loyal brand advocates and buyers.

7
Monthly Reporting & Optimization

Every month you receive a detailed report — reach, impressions, follower growth, top posts, engagement rate, and leads generated. We analyze results and optimize strategy for the next month.

Got Questions?

Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you want to know before starting your social media marketing campaign.

How long does it take to see results from SMM? +
With consistent posting and the right strategy, most businesses start seeing visible follower growth and engagement improvement within 4–6 weeks. Significant results — like viral reels or lead inquiries from social media — typically come in months 2–3. Social media is a long-term brand-building investment, not an overnight fix.
Will you create the content or do I need to provide it? +
We create everything — graphics, reels, captions, hashtags, and stories. We just need your brand logo, product photos/videos (if any), and some basic business information to get started. You review and approve all content before it goes live — you're always in control.
Do you create content in Telugu for local audiences? +
Yes! We create bilingual content in Telugu and English — or pure Telugu — depending on your target audience. For local Hyderabad businesses targeting the general public, Telugu content often gets 2–3× higher engagement and shares compared to English-only posts.
Is paid advertising (Meta Ads) included in the packages? +
The Growth plan includes Meta Ads management with a ₹5,000 ad budget included. The Dominator plan includes full Meta + YouTube Ads management. The Starter plan covers organic content only. Paid ads significantly accelerate growth and lead generation, but excellent results are also achievable with organic content alone.
How many posts do you create per month? +
Starter plan: 12 posts + 4 reels. Growth plan: 20 posts + 8 reels + daily stories. Dominator plan: 30+ posts + 12 reels/shorts + full daily activity across all platforms. We also include story content, carousel posts, and festive/event posts relevant to your business calendar.
Will I have access to my social media accounts? +
Absolutely — your accounts always remain yours. We manage them on your behalf by being added as a manager or editor on your business accounts. You retain full admin control at all times and can view or post independently whenever you want.
Can you grow followers fast using bots or bought followers? +
No — and we never will. Bought followers are fake accounts that don't engage, don't buy, and can get your account penalized or banned by Instagram and Facebook. We grow 100% real, targeted followers who are genuinely interested in your business — slower but far more valuable.
Can I cancel my social media plan anytime? +
Our plans require a minimum 2-month commitment (social media needs consistency to show results), after which you can cancel with 30 days' notice. We don't lock you into long contracts — we earn your continued business through month-after-month results.
