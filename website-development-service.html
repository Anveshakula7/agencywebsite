How We Work

Our Development Process

A clear, transparent 8-step workflow — so you always know what's happening with your project.

1
Requirement Analysis

We start with a discovery call (WhatsApp or phone) to understand your business, goals, target audience, and the features your website needs.

2
Planning & Strategy

We map out the site structure, page layout, content plan, and technology stack. A clear timeline and deliverable list is shared with you upfront.

3
UI/UX Design

We design mobile-first wireframes and visual mockups aligned with your brand colors, fonts, and style. Your feedback shapes the final look.

4
Development

Our developers build the website with clean code — responsive, fast-loading, SEO-structured, and fully functional across all devices and browsers.

5
Testing & QA

Thorough testing across multiple devices, screen sizes, and browsers. We check speed, forms, links, and user experience before you see it.

6
Client Review

We share a live preview link for your review. You can request edits — we revise until you're completely satisfied with every detail.

7
Launch 🚀

Once approved, we deploy to your domain with SSL, connect analytics, and do a final performance check. Your website goes live!

8
Ongoing Maintenance & Support

Post-launch, we offer monthly maintenance plans — updates, security patches, content changes, and speed optimization. You're never alone.

Got Questions?

Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you want to know before starting your website project.

How long does website development take? +
Most business websites are delivered within 7–14 days. Landing pages can be ready in 3–5 days. E-commerce and custom web applications may take 14–30 days depending on complexity. We always share a timeline upfront before starting.
How much does a website cost? +
Our Starter package begins at ₹7,999, Business package at ₹14,999, and Premium (with e-commerce or custom features) at ₹29,999 onwards. The exact cost depends on your requirements — WhatsApp Anvesh for a free, no-obligation quote.
Do you provide hosting and domain? +
Yes! We can assist with domain registration and hosting setup. We recommend reliable providers and help you get everything configured. Hosting costs are typically ₹2,000–₹5,000/year depending on the plan you choose.
Will my website be mobile-friendly? +
100% yes. All our websites are built mobile-first — meaning they're designed for smartphones first, then adapted for tablets and desktops. Over 70% of web traffic in India comes from mobile, so this is non-negotiable for us.
Can I update the website myself? +
If we build your website on WordPress or Shopify, yes — you can update content, add products, and manage your blog without any coding skills. We also provide a short training walkthrough so you feel confident doing it yourself.
Do you provide website maintenance? +
Absolutely. We offer monthly maintenance plans starting at ₹1,999/month — covering security updates, speed optimization, content changes, backups, and uptime monitoring. Premium packages include 1 month free maintenance.
Is SEO included in website development? +
Basic on-page SEO is included in every package — title tags, meta descriptions, heading structure, sitemap, and Google Search Console setup. For full SEO campaigns (keyword ranking, link building, local SEO), we offer a separate SEO service package.
Do you redesign existing websites? +
Yes! Website redesign is one of our most popular services. We take your outdated or slow website and transform it into a modern, fast, mobile-friendly site while preserving your brand identity and existing SEO rankings where possible.
What information do you need to get started? +
To start, we need your business name, logo (if available), brand colors, services/products list, and any reference websites you like. No worries if you don't have everything ready — we'll guide you through it all on a free discovery call.
