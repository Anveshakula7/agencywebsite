The Hidden Dangers

What Happens Without Maintenance?

Ignoring website maintenance is like ignoring car servicing — it always leads to a bigger, costlier breakdown.

🦠
Malware & Hacking

Outdated plugins and themes are the #1 entry point for hackers. One attack can wipe your entire website and customer data.

🐢
Slow Loading Speed

Unoptimized images, bloated code, and no caching make your site slow. Google penalizes slow sites — you lose rankings and visitors.

📉
Google Ranking Drop

Broken links, outdated content, and poor Core Web Vitals cause your rankings to fall — competitors overtake you silently.

🔴
Website Downtime

Server issues, expired SSL, or plugin conflicts can take your site offline. Every minute of downtime = lost leads and revenue.

💔
Broken Forms & Links

Broken contact forms mean lost leads. Dead links frustrate visitors and hurt your SEO score — both are silent revenue killers.

📵
Mobile Issues

Plugin updates and CMS changes can break mobile layouts. Over 70% of your visitors are on mobile — a broken layout = lost customers.

How It Works

Our Maintenance Process

A simple, transparent monthly workflow — so your website is always in perfect health.

1
Free Website Health Check

We start with a comprehensive audit of your website — speed score, security vulnerabilities, broken links, outdated software, uptime history, and mobile responsiveness. You receive a full report before we begin.

2
Onboarding & Access Setup

We securely collect necessary access credentials — hosting cPanel, WordPress admin, or FTP. We set up monitoring tools, backup systems, and security scanners on your website within 24 hours.

3
Initial Cleanup & Optimization

Before ongoing maintenance begins, we fix all existing issues — remove malware, update outdated plugins, optimize speed, clean the database, fix broken links, and renew SSL if needed.

4
24/7 Uptime Monitoring Activated

Your website is now monitored every 5 minutes around the clock. If it goes down for any reason, we receive an instant alert and begin restoring it immediately — often before you even notice.

5
Weekly Routine Tasks

Every week we run security scans, check plugin compatibility, verify form functionality, test page load speed, and ensure your backups are completing successfully without errors.

6
Monthly Deep Maintenance

Full plugin & CMS updates, database optimization, broken link crawl, image compression, Core Web Vitals check, mobile testing, and content updates as per your plan — all done every month.

7
Monthly Report Delivered

At the end of every month, you receive a detailed maintenance report — uptime percentage, speed score before/after, security scan results, tasks completed, and the plan for the next month. Full transparency.

Got Questions?

Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know before choosing a maintenance plan.

Do I really need website maintenance? +
Absolutely. Websites without maintenance face security vulnerabilities (43% of all websites get hacked), slow loading speeds, plugin conflicts, and downtime. Regular maintenance prevents these issues and keeps your website generating leads 24/7 without interruption.
What if my website goes down at night or on a weekend? +
Our monitoring system alerts us instantly — 24 hours a day, 7 days a week, including public holidays. Elite Care clients get a 1-hour emergency response. Pro Care clients get 2-hour response. We don't take days off from monitoring your website.
Will plugin updates break my website? +
Never with us. We test all plugin and CMS updates on a staging/backup copy first. Only after confirming the update is safe do we apply it to your live website. This is our standard process — your live site is never used as a testing ground.
How often are backups taken? +
Daily automated backups for all plans. Backups are stored off-site (not on your server), so even if your hosting server fails, your data is safe. For Pro and Elite plans, we can restore your website to any point within the last 30 days.
Do you maintain non-WordPress websites? +
Yes! We maintain WordPress, WooCommerce, Shopify, custom HTML/CSS websites, PHP sites, React/Next.js applications, and any other platform. Contact us to discuss your specific tech stack and we'll confirm compatibility.
Can I cancel my maintenance plan anytime? +
Yes — no long-term contracts. Cancel anytime with 15 days notice. We don't believe in locking you in. Our clients stay because of the results, not because of contracts. We'll even give you a full backup of your website when you leave.
What access do you need to maintain my website? +
Depending on your site, we need WordPress admin access, cPanel/hosting login, or FTP credentials. All credentials are stored with enterprise-grade security. We only access your website when performing maintenance tasks — never without reason.
Is there a free trial or website health check? +
Yes! We offer a free website health check before you sign up for any plan. We'll analyse your site's speed, security, uptime history, and plugin status, then share a detailed report and our honest recommendation — completely free, no commitment required.
