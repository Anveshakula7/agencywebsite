How We Work

Our Maintenance Process

A simple, transparent onboarding and monthly care process — so you always know what's happening with your website.

1
Free Website Health Checkup

We audit your website's current health — speed score, security status, outdated plugins, broken links, SSL validity, and backup status. You get a clear picture of what needs fixing before we start.

2
Secure Access & Onboarding

We collect necessary credentials (WordPress admin, hosting cPanel, or FTP) through a secure handover. All credentials are stored encrypted and never shared. Your website data is always safe with us.

3
Initial Deep Fix & Optimization

In the first week, we fix all existing issues — update outdated software, resolve bugs, improve speed, install security tools, and set up automated daily backups. Your website gets a complete health reset.

4
24/7 Monitoring Setup

We configure uptime monitoring, security scanning, and backup systems to run automatically in the background. If anything breaks or is attacked, we get notified instantly and take action.

5
Monthly Maintenance Tasks

Every month we run the full checklist — plugin updates, speed audit, security scan, broken link check, database cleanup, and any content updates you've requested. Consistent, proactive care every month.

6
On-Demand Bug Fixes & Support

Between monthly tasks, you can WhatsApp Anvesh anytime to report an issue. Most bugs are fixed within 24 hours. For critical issues (site down, payment gateway broken), we respond within 2–4 hours.

7
Monthly Report & Review

Every month you receive a detailed report — tasks completed, speed scores, security threats blocked, backups taken, and uptime stats. Plus a plan for the next month if any improvements are needed.

Got Questions?

Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you want to know before handing your website care to us.

Do you maintain websites built by other developers? +
Yes, absolutely. We maintain websites built by any developer on any platform — WordPress, custom PHP, HTML/CSS, WooCommerce, and more. As long as we have access credentials, we can take over maintenance immediately. We'll do a free health check first to understand the current state of your website.
Is my website data safe with you? +
100% yes. All credentials you share are stored securely and encrypted. We sign a confidentiality agreement upon request. We only access your website when performing agreed maintenance tasks and never share your data with any third party. Your website and business data are completely safe with us.
What if my website gets hacked while on your plan? +
On our Pro and Elite plans, hack recovery is fully covered. We detect the breach through our security monitoring, clean the malware, remove backdoors, restore from backup, and harden security to prevent reoccurrence — at no extra cost. Elite plan clients get 2–4 hour emergency response, Pro plan within 24 hours.
How quickly do you fix bugs or issues I report? +
Basic plan: within 48 hours. Pro plan: within 24 hours. Elite plan: within 2–4 hours for critical issues (site down, payment broken) and within 24 hours for non-critical bugs. You simply WhatsApp Anvesh with the issue and we handle it — no long support tickets or call queues.
What does "content update request" mean? +
Content updates include: changing text on any page, adding/removing products, updating prices, replacing images, adding a new blog post, updating contact details, adding a new page, or any other content change on your existing website. Basic plan includes 2 requests/month, Pro plan 5, Elite plan unlimited.
Do you only maintain WordPress websites? +
We specialize in WordPress and WooCommerce maintenance, which covers 90%+ of business websites in Hyderabad. We also maintain custom HTML/CSS websites, PHP websites, and sites on platforms like Wix and Shopify. Contact us with your platform details for a customized plan.
Will plugin updates break my website? +
This is a genuine risk when updates are done carelessly. We always take a full backup before running any updates, test in a staging environment when possible, and verify your site is fully functional after every update. If an update does cause an issue, we roll back and fix it immediately — at no extra charge.
Can I cancel my maintenance plan anytime? +
Our plans require a minimum 2-month commitment, after which you can cancel with 30 days' notice. We don't lock you into long contracts — we earn your continued trust by keeping your website in excellent shape every single month.
