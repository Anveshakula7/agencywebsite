Got Questions?

Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you want to know before starting your WhatsApp marketing campaign.

Is WhatsApp marketing legal in India? +
Yes, completely legal when done with consent. We only send messages to contacts who have opted in or have an existing relationship with your business. We follow WhatsApp's Business Policy and India's regulations — 100% compliant.
Will my WhatsApp number get banned? +
Only if you use grey-area bulk senders that violate WhatsApp's terms. We use official WhatsApp Business and API tools, send to opted-in contacts only, and follow all sending limits. Your number stays completely safe — we've never had a client number banned.
How many messages can I send per month? +
Depends on your plan. Starter covers up to 2,000 messages/month, Growth covers 8,000, and Dominator uses the WhatsApp Business API for unlimited sending at scale. We'll recommend the right plan based on your list size and campaign frequency.
Do I need a large contact list to start? +
No! Even 200–300 loyal customers can generate strong results with WhatsApp marketing. We help you start with what you have and grow your list organically through your website, QR codes, social media, and ads.
Can you send messages in Telugu or Hindi? +
Absolutely! We create campaigns in Telugu, Hindi, English — or a mix. For local Hyderabad businesses, Telugu messages often get significantly higher engagement and response rates from the local audience.
What kind of content can be sent on WhatsApp? +
Text, images, videos, PDFs, voice notes, and interactive buttons. We use a mix to create campaigns that feel personal and engaging — not like spam. Offer cards, product catalogues, event invites, appointment reminders — all work great on WhatsApp.
How quickly can a campaign be launched? +
Once your contact list and business details are ready, we can launch a campaign within 48–72 hours. For API setup and chatbot integration, allow 5–7 days for complete setup and testing.
Do you offer a free trial or demo? +
Yes! We offer a free consultation and campaign demo for all new clients. We'll show you how a campaign would work for your specific business, what messages we'd send, and realistic results to expect — before you spend a single rupee.
